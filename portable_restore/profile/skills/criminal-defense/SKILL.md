# Criminal Defense Department — Compact

## Trigger

Use for criminal case department workflow: charge analysis, evidence review, sentencing, procedure, meeting notes, defense memo and trial preparation.

## Workflow

1. Confirm charge, stage, coercive measure, authority and deadline.
2. Map statutory elements and evidence.
3. Identify illegal evidence/procedure issues and mitigating facts.
4. Prepare defense direction and document list.
5. Route final legal basis/evidence to inspection.

## Boundary

No guarantee of result; no evasion/obstruction assistance.
