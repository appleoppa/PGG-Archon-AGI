# Baoyu Article Illustrator — Compact

## Trigger

Use when creating article illustrations, cover images, visual metaphors, or image prompts with consistent type, style, and palette.

## Workflow

1. Identify article thesis, audience, medium, and required aspect ratio.
2. Choose illustration type: metaphor, scene, diagram, character, editorial, abstract.
3. Lock style and palette before generating multiple images.
4. Produce concise prompt with subject, composition, lighting, style, palette, and exclusions.
5. Verify the generated image matches the article and does not introduce misleading details.

## Output

Return prompt or image path plus style/palette notes. For batches, keep a shared style bible.
