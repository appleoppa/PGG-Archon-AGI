# Sports Master Department — Compact

## Trigger

Use for sports law,赛事/俱乐部/运动员合同、赞助、纪律处罚、体育仲裁、竞彩合规与风险分析。

## Workflow

1. Classify issue: contract, discipline, transfer, sponsorship, IP, injury, betting/compliance.
2. Identify governing rules: national law, association rules, league rules, arbitration clause.
3. Map facts, evidence, deadlines and remedies.
4. Produce legal/risk memo; for betting-related matters, keep compliance and responsible-risk boundary.

## Boundary

No gambling guarantee or illegal betting assistance.
