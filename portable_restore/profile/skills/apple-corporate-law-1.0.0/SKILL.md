# Apple Corporate Law — Compact

## Trigger

Use for company governance, shareholder disputes, equity transfer, board/shareholder resolutions, compliance, capital contribution, veil piercing and corporate documents.

## Workflow

1. Identify company type, shareholders, governance body and dispute/transaction goal.
2. Check articles of association, resolutions, capital contribution, registration and authority.
3. Map legal risks and document requirements.
4. Draft/review resolutions, agreements, opinions or compliance checklist.
