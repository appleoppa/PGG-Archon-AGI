# Apple Real Estate Development Law — Compact

## Trigger

Use for land acquisition, development, planning, construction, sale, leasing, property management, urban renewal, project compliance and real-estate dispute analysis.

## Workflow

1. Identify project stage: land → planning → construction → pre-sale/sale → delivery → property.
2. Check approvals, permits, contracts, payment, performance, defects and regulatory risk.
3. Map stakeholders: developer, contractor, buyer, government, bank, property company.
4. Verify legal basis and local policy where needed.
5. Produce risk list and action checklist.

## Must check

Land use right, planning permit, construction permit, pre-sale permit, completion acceptance, delivery conditions, mortgage/encumbrance, defect evidence and limitation period.
