# Non-Litigation Department — Compact

## Trigger

Use for bankruptcy/restructuring, M&A, compliance, due diligence, corporate governance, contract projects and other non-litigation matters.

## Workflow

1. Identify project type, parties, goal, timeline and deliverables.
2. Build due diligence/checklist and document request list.
3. Spot legal/commercial/compliance risks.
4. Draft/review transaction documents or legal memo.
5. Send high-risk legal conclusions to audit/inspection.
