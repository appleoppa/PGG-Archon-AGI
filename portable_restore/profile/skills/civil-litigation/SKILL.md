# Civil Litigation Department — Compact

## Trigger

Use for民商事、建工、仲裁案件办理，包括起诉、答辩、证据、庭审、调解和执行前准备。

## Workflow

1. Confirm cause of action, parties, stage, court/arbitration and deadlines.
2. Build claim/defense elements and burden of proof.
3. Request legal support for authority and evidence department for evidence catalogue.
4. Prepare pleadings/applications/hearing outline.
5. Send to inspection/audit before external delivery.
