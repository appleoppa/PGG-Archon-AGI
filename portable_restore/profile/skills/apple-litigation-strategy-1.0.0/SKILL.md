# Apple Litigation Strategy — Compact

## Trigger

Use for litigation route selection, claim design, evidence strategy, settlement leverage, preservation, counterparty prediction and cost-benefit decision.

## Workflow

1. Define objective and acceptable outcomes.
2. Evaluate facts, legal basis, evidence, procedure, enforcement and opponent.
3. Compare strategies: sue/defend/settle/preserve/negotiate/arbitrate.
4. Choose strategy with risk, cost, timeline and fallback plan.
