# Apple Criminal Defense — Compact

## Trigger

Use for criminal defense strategy, charge analysis, sentencing, evidence exclusion, confession review, investigation/prosecution/trial stages, and compliance-related criminal risk.

## Workflow

1. Identify charge, stage, compulsory measures and key deadlines.
2. Map statutory elements, facts, evidence and burden.
3. Review legality of evidence, confession, procedure and mitigating factors.
4. Build defense: innocence, lesser charge, sentencing mitigation, procedural objections, settlement/understanding where applicable.
5. Mark lawyer review and procedural risk boundaries.
