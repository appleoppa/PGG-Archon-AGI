# Apple Bankruptcy — Compact

## Trigger

Use for bankruptcy liquidation, reorganization, settlement, creditor claims, administrator duties, asset disposal, insolvency litigation and restructuring plans.

## Workflow

1. Identify procedure: application, acceptance, claim declaration, creditors meeting, reorganization/liquidation.
2. Check debtor assets/debts, secured claims, employee/tax claims, pending litigation and avoidance actions.
3. Map stakeholder interests and statutory deadlines.
4. Produce claim review, restructuring/liquidation strategy or risk memo.
