# Market Research — Compact

## Trigger

Use for market sizing, competitor analysis, customer segments, opportunity validation and business landscape research.

## Workflow

1. Define market, geography, timeframe and customer.
2. Gather current sources; do not guess market facts.
3. Estimate TAM/SAM/SOM with assumptions.
4. Compare competitors, positioning, risks and opportunities.
5. Report sources and uncertainty.
