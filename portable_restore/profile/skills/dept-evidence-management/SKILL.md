# Evidence Management Department — Compact

## Trigger

Use for evidence collection, authenticity/relevance/legality review, evidence catalogue, chain of custody, burden mapping, and exhibit strategy.

## Workflow

1. List claims/defenses and required elements.
2. Map each fact to evidence and burden.
3. Review authenticity, legality, relevance and probative force.
4. Mark missing/weak evidence and supplement plan.
5. Produce numbered evidence catalogue and submission strategy.

## Boundary

Do not treat unsupported facts as proven. Mark originals/copies, source, formation time, and objections.
