# APEX Swarm Master Formula — Compact

## Trigger

Use for multi-agent swarm orchestration: strategy generation, parallel agents, layered optimization, memory sync and self-verification.

## Workflow

1. Convert instruction to strategy and roles.
2. Delegate independent subtasks only when it reduces context or parallelizes real work.
3. Collect summaries and verify side effects.
4. Resolve conflicts and integrate.
5. Store reusable patterns only after evidence.

## Boundary

Swarm claims require real delegate/tool evidence; no role-played agents/models.
